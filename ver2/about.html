ABOUT THE AUTHOR OF THIS SITE

I am a home maker and live in Secunderabad, Telangana, India.
Young women are focussing their time and energy on studies and job and find little time to learn cooking. As a result they are not ready to manage the kitchen by the time they are married. They want to start with simple recipes that are easy to pick up and less time consuming. I want my website to be helpful to them.
